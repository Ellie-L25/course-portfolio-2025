My Top 5 Favorite Flowers

  1. Roses

    They are a classic and elegant selection of flowers. They were always my favorite at my aunt's shop.

  2. Sunflowers

    Sunflowers are a bright and tall, they always have a way to brighten my day.

  3. Tulips

    I love the many colors that tulips bring during the spring time!

  4. Hydrangeas

    The soft shades of blue, pink, and white, they carry a peaceful feel.

  5. Daisies

    They are simple and sweet, they always remind me of sunny and fun days!
